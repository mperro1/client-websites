About Us


At YEAH, we are committed to the support of out local home-education families. Through outreach and financial assistance, we strive to reduce stress and strengthen the educational bond made at home.

You Can Help!


With a 501c3 non-profit, you can feel confident that your donations will be used with utmost care. We encourage you to talk with your spouse or family about what you can do to help. Here are some suggestions

  • Sponsor a child
  • Volunteer at events
  • Teach your special skill
  • Tutor core subjects
  • Donate books
  • Contact us for more!
